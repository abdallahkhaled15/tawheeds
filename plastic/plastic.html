Plastic Services

Al Tawhied Company leads the regional leadership in this industry, which is intertwined with the sector of preparing and forming plastics within Al Tawhied Company. Al Tawhied Company's plastic forming sector is distinguished by unconventional innovation and years of experience, which can be seen in its blowing and injection products.
